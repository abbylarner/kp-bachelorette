What is Design Like Mad?

It’s an event unlike any other where 12 hours of sheer design madness produces amazing work for local non-profit organizations. This “design marathon” brings together student and professional designers who provide pro-bono design work for deserving non-profit organizations so they can continue to help Seattle.
